# Compliance Control Notes

Use current official sources for production decisions. This file is a routing guide for what to verify.

## Product-Type Routing

| Product signal | Verify |
|---|---|
| Electrical, plug, charger, battery, LED, USB | PSE/PSC applicability, dangerous goods, battery/FBA restrictions |
| Food contact, kitchen, container, tableware | food-contact or household goods labeling, material proof, Japan label requirements |
| Food, supplement, drink | category approval, food labeling, importer obligations, claims restrictions |
| Cosmetic, quasi-drug, medical, health | PMD Act routing, import/marketing authorization, claims restrictions |
| Baby/child/pet safety restraint | safety standards, age/pet-size claims, choking/load/chemical proof |
| Outdoor load-bearing, fire, sharp, gas, chemical | safety certification, dangerous goods, prohibited/restricted item review |
| Brand compatibility | trademark ownership, compatibility wording approval, competitor ad policy |

## Claim Ledger

Every claim should have:
- claim text
- allowed placement: title/bullet/description/A+/image/ad
- proof required
- proof available
- status: pass/needs_proof/blocked

Block risky claims when proof is missing:
- medical, therapeutic, pain relief, weight loss
- antibacterial, anti-mold, anti-odor, waterproof, fireproof
- food-grade, BPA-free, child-safe, pet-safe
- No.1, best, cheapest, official, authorized
- origin, certification, durability, load-bearing

## Evidence Examples

- Seller Central category/restricted product screenshot.
- JAN/GTIN ownership or exemption confirmation.
- Brand Registry status or authorization letter.
- Supplier spec sheet and test report.
- Product label artwork in Japanese.
- Import invoice, packing list, HS code, IOR plan.
- FBA dangerous goods or restriction review.
- FBA fee estimate and cost calculator output.
